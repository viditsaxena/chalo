Unwander is Travel Plans

We did not just make another travel planning app. Our goal was to make something supremely simple for people to create travel plans and we have done just that. No million options and cluttered screens. Just name your trip and you are off.

Finally what you want

We made it really, really simple for you to add interesting locations to a travel plan. Once you add our Clipper, a chrome extension, you can right click on any point of interest or location on any website and simply add it to your travel plans. We will pull in all the details like address, photos, opening hours, tips etc.






Focused

See all your clips on a map, just like you’d expect. Want to divide the clips for different days? Just drag and drop them. Add commentary if you like. And that's it......Just a simple travel plan and not a lot of confusing options.
